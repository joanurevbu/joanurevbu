### Hi, I'm Joan Urevbu 👋

**Education technologist & writer** — I build the quiet systems that keep schools running.

I work at the seam between education and software: timetabling, parent
communication, school-gate logistics, and SMS messaging infrastructure built for
low-bandwidth, high-trust environments. Based between Benin City 🇳🇬 and Porto
Alegre 🇧🇷. Also known as **Joan Osunde**.

- 🏫 I build school-management & parent-messaging systems used by institutions every day
- ✍️ I write about technology in African classrooms and designing for low bandwidth
